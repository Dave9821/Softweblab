Everything you need to know about working with SoftWebLab.

What IT services does SoftWebLab provide? +

SoftWebLab provides a full suite of IT services including web development, mobile app development (iOS & Android), digital marketing, social media marketing (SMM), IT managed services, and dedicated IT support for small and medium businesses in Chicago, IL.

How much does a website cost? +

Our website development starts at $500 for a basic business website. E-commerce platforms, custom web applications, and enterprise solutions are priced based on your specific requirements. We offer a free consultation and quote — contact us at [email protected] or call +1 312 452 3784.

Do you provide IT support for small businesses? +

Yes! SoftWebLab specializes in IT support for small and medium-sized businesses. Our services include helpdesk support, on-site technical visits, hardware and software setup, Office 365 management, network configuration, and cybersecurity — at a fraction of the cost of an in-house IT team.

How long does it take to build a website or app? +

A standard business website typically takes 2–4 weeks. Mobile app development ranges from 6–12 weeks depending on complexity. We always provide a clear project timeline before starting, with regular progress updates throughout the build.

Where is SoftWebLab located? +

SoftWebLab is headquartered at 302 West Lane, Chicago, IL. We serve clients across Chicago and the greater United States, both in-person and remotely. Reach us at [email protected] or +1 312 452 3784.
